Pricing & Scope

How projects are scoped

Pricing depends on scope and complexity, not just time. The same service category can vary depending on how many stakeholders are involved, how much documentation is required, and whether the work includes workflow, dashboard, or automation structure.

Mid-scope projects

Typical range: ₩1M – ₩3M

  • Single team or relatively contained workflow
  • Focused sprint with defined output
  • Strategy memo, structure proposal, or light process redesign

High-scope projects

Typical range: ₩3M – ₩15M+

  • Multiple stakeholders or cross-functional collaboration
  • Broader onboarding / operations redesign
  • Dashboard, reporting, or automation structure included
  • Higher documentation depth and implementation alignment

In practice, projects are priced based on complexity, timeline, number of teams involved, and the depth of deliverables required.
